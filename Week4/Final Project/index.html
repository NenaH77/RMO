Life is Sweet!

At Cupcake Dreams Inc, we offer professional classes for those interested in making their own business as well as non conventional classes for those looking to enjoy time with friends and family.

Baking

Our classes are 100% professional. We teach the importance of mixing certain ingredients and decorating. With our on-site and online classes, we make it possible to accommodate your schedule

Family Memories

Creating a fun family day or a girls night out? Join us for a private class where we provide the supplies and clean up the mess left behind. While you are enjoying time with the family we offer special complimentary wine, soda, milk and fruit juice.
